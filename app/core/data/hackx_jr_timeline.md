# hackX Jr. Timeline

## Timeline and Important Dates

**hackX Jr. Schedule**
- **Registrations Open**: 3 July
  - Registration begins for schools and young innovators to embark on their journey.
- **Awareness Session**: 31 July
  - An introductory session for students and teachers covering the competition, timeline, and what it takes to succeed.
- **Proposal Submission**: 1 August
  - Teams submit their innovative ideas and proposed solutions before the proposal submission deadline.
- **Online Workshop Series 1**: 26 August
  - A series of expert-led workshops designed to strengthen innovation, ideation, and solution development.
- **InnoX (Semi-Finals)**: 3 October
  - Selected teams pitch their ideas to a panel of judges for the opportunity to advance to the Grand Finals.
- **Online Workshop Series 2**: 13 October
  - Advanced workshops focused on refining prototypes, presentations, and pitching skills ahead of the finals.
- **Grand Finals**: 11 November
  - The nation's top finalists present their innovations on the national stage and compete for the championship title.

**Important Notes regarding the Timeline:**
- Dates are tentative and subject to change. Always refer to the official website (hackxjr.lk) for any updates.
